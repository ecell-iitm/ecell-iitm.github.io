Nirmaan is the pre-incubation cell of IIT Madras

There are a lot of students in institute who have ideas for startups. A lot of students who want to work on startups. But for doing that, there is a huge jump which teams need to make to get into the incubated. They require the product, the business model and a good team. We understand that it’s not possible to take such a drastic jump by ourselves and hence, launched this pre-incubation cell to take students from idea-stage to startup stage.

"Experience Entrepreneurship @ IITM"

Why
Nirmaan?

1) Setting up a long term program to facilitate the students to think beyond just placements!

 

2) To prepare the students to graduate to the Incubation Cell at IIT Madras and grow further by giving them a feel of a start-up as well as providing seed funding for their projects.

 

What is in it for You?

Funding upto 2 Lakhs INR

Office space in CFI and many other workshops and initiatives.

Network with alumni and entrepreneurs often coming to Insti

Mentorship will be provided to all the teams of co-founders working on their product for both, technical guidance and over business aspects of starting up. Collaborations with TiE, RTBI, Incubation Cell and Villgro to organise workshops on finance and business of starting up.

A series of conferences and networking opportunities are available exclusively to the participants, with top-notch entrepreneurs and venture capitalists at events planned throughout the year.

Participation and successfully clearing 2 rounds of funding enables the team members to potentially convert their participation into a 3-credit free elective.

How to join the program?

  • The program is open to current students of all degree programs of IIT Madras. You must have completed atleast 2 years (1 year for M-Tech and MBA students).
  • Brilliant ideas from exceptional second year students will also be accepted on case-to-case basis.
  • Startup ideas falling under one of the below themes of national importance will be prefered:
    • • Education Technogy
    • • Fin Tech
    • • Healthcare
    • • Water and Sanitation
    • • Waste management
    • • Energy
    • • Agriculture
    • • Transportation and environment
    • • Affordable housing
    • • Assistive technology
