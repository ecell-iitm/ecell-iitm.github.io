Panel Discussion

A panel discussion is aimed at exposing the audience to different view points on an entrepreneurial topic. It includes speakers who are experts in the relevant fields educate students about the relevant topic and in the process of discussion, point out the differences and similarities with other topics. With the help of a moderator, such sessions not only put forward a healthy debate with much food for thought, but also bring out the uniqueness in every aspect of entrepreneurship as discussed by the personalities involved.
